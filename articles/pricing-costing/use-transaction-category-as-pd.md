## <a name="set-up-the-transaction-category-field-as-a-pricing-dimension"></a><span data-ttu-id="b46cc-168">Podešavanje polja „Kategorija transakcije“ kao dimenzije određivanja cena</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="b46cc-168">Set up the Transaction Category field as a pricing dimension</span></span>

1. <span data-ttu-id="b46cc-169">Idite na **Podešavanja** > **Parametri**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="b46cc-169">Go to **Settings** > **Parameters**.</span></span> 
2. <span data-ttu-id="b46cc-170">Na stranici **Parametri**, na kartici **Dimenzije za određivanje cena zasnovane na iznosima**, potvrdite da li mreža prikazuje zapise u entitetu **Dimenzije za određivanje cena**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="b46cc-170">On the **Parameters** page, on the **Amount-Based Pricing Dimensions** tab, verify that the grid shows the records in the **Pricing Dimensions** entity.</span></span>
3. <span data-ttu-id="b46cc-171">Dodajte **Kategorija transakcije** na ovu listu i podesite polja **Primenljivo na troškove** i **Primenjivo na prodaju** na **Da**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="b46cc-171">Add **Transaction Category** to this list and set the **Applicable to Cost** and **Applicable to Sale** fields to **Yes**.</span></span>
4. <span data-ttu-id="b46cc-172">U polju **Vrsta dimenzije** izaberite **Zasnovano na iznosu**, a zatim izaberite prioritet za stavku **Kategorija transakcije** jer je povezana sa troškovima i prodajom.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="b46cc-172">In the **Dimension Type** field, select **Amount-based**, and then select the priority for **Transaction Category** as it relates to cost and sales.</span></span>
